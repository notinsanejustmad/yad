Sunrise

Long was it dark, long time it's cold.
Great big robes, they seem so old.
Long lost hope, no sign of new,
when come the times that we once knew?

Oh, it breaks, the robe so black.
I see fire through that crack.
Light shines through, upon our lands,
As they rise, they send their hands.

Oh, a head of fire red,
Heat and colour it has fed,
dressed in robes in colours blue,
to us: new love the old withdrew.

Hour by hour, they rise so fast,
on the old a curse have cast.
All of us, we watch and nod,
we welcome it, the birth of god.
