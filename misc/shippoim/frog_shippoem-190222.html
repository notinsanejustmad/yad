Frog Shippoem

Little legs so cute and green,
the cutest I have ever seen.
you get ready, jump up high,
I think you might just reach the sky.

Smallest eyes so cute and brown,
the smallest I have ever known.
I look at you and you look back,
you pierce my soul, know what I lack.

Bodies of such brilliant hues,
such great yellows, the best of blues.
content smile once you get food,
with tenderness I boop your snoot.
