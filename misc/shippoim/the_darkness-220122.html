The Darkness

dark eyes
silently looking
out of the window
stare into the night,
seeking something,
but finding nothing but darkness.

The night
black as ink,
moving along,
as I move,
for ever,
engulfing all that is here.

moving along:
the darkness,
filling my head,
like a bottle
of dark wine.
my thoughts swim slowly.

I am gone.
I am lost,
in darkness.
what happened
to me?
Where am I now?
