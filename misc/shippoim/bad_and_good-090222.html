Bad and Good

On a day that's just like this
I walk my way monotonously.
Hoping for a day of bliss
I walk listening to music.

The music good, my pace is fast,
I think as I walk in sorrow,
the faster it is the better at last,
and the faster I'll meet my demise.

And In my head the thoughts so rich
oh so many, I lose my count.
I hear them all for I cant choose which,
and all are equally horrible.

But as I walk I understand it soon
the bad wins over good,
like the thoughts over my tune
or bad lines in a good poem.
