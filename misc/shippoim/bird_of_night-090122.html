Bird of Night

Day is sleeping, dark and silent,
night is watching over me.
Long I watch the woods so quiet,
wait for anything I see.
Black and glossy feathers flutter
of a bird as black as night.
Struck by awe and admiration
by this beauty of a sight
want to go in desperation
to the bird, a blinding light.
just like that, I see it shudder
the bird leaves, it used its flight.
