Second Time

first one, then two, then three, then four.
Five passing through, then six: there's more.
They carry time, they carry power:
thousands pass within an hour.

broken metal, rotten food,
crooked backs and changing mood.
For all our efforts, all the fuss,
this is what they do to us.

The first, the second, one a time,
just ignored it feels just fine.
Minutes, seconds, one more time,
i sit here and find a rhyme.
