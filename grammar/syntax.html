Word order

The general word order in yad is SOV, but it can be changed to OVS to mark passive voice. Adjectives come after nouns, and adverbs after verbs. Tense is marked with particles before the verb, and conjunctions generally come in the first position. The indirect object always comes after the verb.

Adjectives and adverbs

Any adjective in yad can be used as an adverb and vice versa. Adjectives in yad inflect for gender and are positioned directly after the noun the describe. Adverbs agree in gender with the verb.

on isyela isye -net
