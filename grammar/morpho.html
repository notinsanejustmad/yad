Morphology

Jad is an agglutinative language with isolating parts and a tendency towards suffixed, with the evidentials being the only prefixes.

The copula

The copula in yad is don and can be roughly translated to english "to be". It has a variety of uses, but mainly it is use to denote that the subject is the object. It is the only verb that can take adjectives as object, as seen in the following examples.

onisyelaisye-nidon-oj
CLFcatsilent-ABSCOP-A
"The cat is silent"
zin koraisyela-ninefdon-et
Koracat-ABSNEGCOP-N
"Kora is not a cat"

Noun Cases

Jad has seven cases:
Ergative, Absolutive, Nominative, Posessive, Commitative, Ornative and Locative.
The order of these cases is ERG/ABS/NOM-COM-LOC-ORN. The Commitative and Ornative have the same suffix, but still can be distinguished by an "epenthetic affix" which will be explained in the following.

Ergative

The subject of a verb in a transitive sentence takes the ergative case, which remains unmarked. It also marks the subject on a reflexive verb.

i-∅onisyela-nighim-ic
1SG-ERGCLFcat-ABSlove-F
"I love the cat"
i-∅cor-ic
1SG-ERGspeak-F
"I encourage myself"

Absolutive

The absolutive case marks the subject of an intransitive verb or the object of a transitive verb. It is marked by the suffix -ni. The /n/ is dropped after nasals and unvoiced consonants.

iisyela-nighim-ic
1SGcat-ABSlove-F
"I love the cat"
i-nicor-ic
1SG-ABSspeak-F
"I speak"

Nominative

The nominative marks the subject of an transitive or intransitive subordinate sentence. The suffix for this case is -far. The /f/ is dropped after stops, nasals, /f/ and /v/.

ionisyela-nighim-icsemild-arighim-ojnef
1SGCLFcat-ABSlove-Fbut3SG-NOM1SGlove-ANEG
"I love the cat but it doesn't love me"
ionisyela-nighim-icsemild-arow-kaj
1SGCLFcat-ABSlove-Fbut3SG-NOMtravel-A
"I love the cat but it runs (away)"

Comitative

The comitative is used to express something similiar to the english word "with" and is marked by the suffix -dog, although the /d/ is dropped after consonants that aren't approximants. The comitative case can also function as a Instrumental case.

iisyela-ni-dogow-ic
1SGcat-ABS-COMtravel-F
"I travel with a cat"
